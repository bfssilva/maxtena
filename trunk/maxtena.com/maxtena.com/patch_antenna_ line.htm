The Maxtena GPS patch antenna line is introduced, Blacksburg VA

Maxtena introduces four new GPS antennas to the product line. The MPA series includes four ceramic Microstrip Patch Antennas with high efficiency and realized gain. The Maxtena Patch Antenna series is a low profile compact ceramic antenna designed for 1575.42 MHz L1 GPS applications. The Antenna incorporates a flat rectangular micro-strip, which is designed for GPS right-hand circular polarization wave reception. The patch antennas are available in the following sizes: 25x25mm, 18x18mm, 15x15mm,and 12x12mm. Each antenna features an adhesive tape and a connector pin on the back for easy installation on different surfaces. Tuning kits are also available for all patch antennas. They include 10 of the same size patch antennas tuned at 2 MHz frequency steps. This enables the customer to select the most efficient antenna and frequency they need. Maxtena’s line of passive patch antennas is ideal for handheld & stationary GPS devices. Maxtena offers complete antenna solutions to meet the specialized customer needs.
