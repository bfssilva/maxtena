Maxtena, Inc. Introduces Viking SatCom as a New Distribution Partner for Asia and South America, Blacksburg VA, 06/2010

Maxtena, Inc. today announced that Viking SatCom will act as Maxtena's distributor for Africa, Asia and South America.

Viking SatCom is a leading manufacturer and distributor of both consumer and commercial satellite antenna products. Viking Satcom offers a "One Stop Shop" approach to system integrators and resellers through an extensive product line consisting of high quality satellite antennas, RF peripheral equipment, and wireless communication products. With Corporate offices located in Albion, Michigan, Viking SatCom offers a complete line of satellite products, communication systems, and accessories at cost effective prices, making Viking SatCom your most trusted and experienced provider of satellite equipment.

"We are very excited about the distribution agreement between Viking SatCom and Maxtena, and feel that the state of the art antenna products supplied by Maxtena complement our extensive line of satellite communication products", said Paul Wagler President of Viking SatCom. "As our industry maximizes on ways to deliver communication products and services, the Maxtena product line allows Viking Satcom to continue with our goal of offering cutting edge solutions to our customers."

Maxtena, Inc. has been working very closely with Viking SatCom in the past and is very proud to have signed Viking SatCom as the new distribution partner for Africa, Asia and South America. "Maxtena will continue to pursue new opportunities and markets throughout the world, and with Viking SatCom we have the right partnership to execute this goal" said Maxtena’s Vice President & Coo Mr. Wayne Telman. Viking SatCom will be showcasing Maxtena’s newest products at this year’s CommunicAsia 2010 – June 15-18, 2010 in Singapore, Booth number 6F1-09. Any question regarding the show please contact vanja.maric@maxtena.com.

Maxtena® is a research, development, and manufacturing company offering both standard and custom antenna products for a variety of applications with an emphasis on Mobile Satellite Services and high performance GPS antennas. Maxtena® offers Helicore® Technology, Precision GPS (L1/L2), and Smart-X™ phased-array antennas (12dBi performance with Omni-directional coverage) with 3-axis stabilization for global asset tracking. Maxtena® provides customer-tailored research for antenna element design, antenna array design, propagation effects, system design and engineering services.
