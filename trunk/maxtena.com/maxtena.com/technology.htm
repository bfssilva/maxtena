Advanced Technology
Maxtena employs advanced technology and offers innovative antenna designs for GPS, satellite radio and ISM markets.
FilterMAX™
Maxtena has created a proprietary filter technology that does not add any insertion loss to the signal path. The customer can define any filter shape required to block out of band signals without hurting the receiver's overall sensitivity.
Imagine attenuating out of band blockers by 40dB and passing in band signals without any additional loss. The customer can then add another 40dB of gain to the front end LNA boosting sensitivity and lowering noise figure.
PolarMAX™
Maxtena has a proprietary tuning technology that allows Maxtena designers to shift the resonant frequency of the antenna together with the center frequency of the narrowband matching network. This allows Maxtena designs to achieve the lowest axial ratio in the industry ~0.2dB. (Axial Ratio is a standard measure of quality of circularly polarized signals used by satellites.)
RadioMAX™
Maxtena has designed an enhanced version of its antennas that adds 2-3dB to the overall sensitivity of the receiver and protects the antenna from stray signals coming from the handset. These interfering signals are converted into common mode noise and can be easily removed by the customer's receiver. RadioMAX™ differential technology allows for integration directly to the RFIC enabling the customer to have dramatically reduced Bill of Materials, lower cost, and the same performance without an external LNA.
