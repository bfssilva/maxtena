Antenna System Conference, Austin TX

Maxtena’s CEO & President Dr. Stanislav Licul presented his research on differential interface antennas vs. single ended antennas. Through direct comparison of the two platforms Dr. Licul was able to illustrate how a differential interface antenna can lead to better performance, and better noise reduction. The direct evaluation concludes that the differntial interface antenna has higher gain, better human body immunity and outstanding performance compared to single ended antennas.
