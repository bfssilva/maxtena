Antennas and Radio Systems Through Innovative Research, Development, and Manufacturing

Maxtena offers a variety of engineering & testing solutions and services.
Measurements

Maxtena offers testing services for a variety of antenna measurements using its own spherical anechoic chamber covering 800MHz-18GHz.
Simulations

Maxtena uses CST’s Microwave Studio and NEC to simulate complex antennas and environments. This shortens the design cycle time and provides optimum custom solutions.
Embedded Solutions

Maxtena has experience and expertise in complex antenna integration. Some project examples include: five-antenna integration in mobile phones for GSM, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and GPS applications; GPS antenna integration into personal and security navigation devices.
Active/Modal Solutions

Maxtena has in-depth experience with active designs, including LNA’s, PA’s, and filtering. Maxtena can supply full systems and subsystems such as phased array transceivers.
Special Capabilities
  • Satellite link analysis
  • Antenna studies
  • Propagation studies
  • Embedded hardware and software
  • Custom solutions
Manufacturing Capabilities
Maxtena has the manufacturing expertise and capabilities. Using custom proprietary technology Maxtena developed and industry unique manufacturing method to achieve unprecedented product yield, reduce cost and high volume production capabilities.
Compliance
Maxtena ensures compliance with standards such as FCC15, FCC25, ETSI, MIL461, and MIL 8-10F requirements.
