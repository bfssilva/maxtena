TechNite Awards: The NewVA Corridor Technology Council (NCTC), Nomination for Innovation Award, Roanoke, VA, 04/2009

Maxtena is pleased to announce their nomination for the 2009 TechNite Award for Innovation. The TechNite awards commemorate local industry leaders in the New River Valley. The Innovation Award recognizes an individual, a group of individuals, company or other organization that sets the standard for thought, leadership, and innovation in their field. The award signals doing something new or different, and doing it successfully. The innovation the company makes is seen as a technological breakthrough or great market opportunity. The winners of the award will be announced at the TechNite Awards Banquet May 21st in the Hotel Roanoke and Conference Center from 5:30pm-9pm. The Banquet will be celebrating five categories: Rising Star, Entrepreneur, NewVa Leadership Innovation, and Educator. To hear more about the TechNite Awards visit: http://www.thetechnologycouncil.com/TechNite.
