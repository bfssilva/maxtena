Maxtena, Inc. Introduces Karl Kruse GmbH as a New Distribution Partner, Blacksburg VA, 05/2010

Maxtena, Inc. today announced that Karl Kruse GmbH & Co KG will act as Maxtena's distributor for Western Europe and Asia.

Karl Kruse GmbH & Co KG is a leading supply chain partner for technology–driven companies operating from Germany, in Europe and the whole World. They provide solutions in electronic components distributions and reverse supply chain management to high-tech CEM and OEM and other companies operating in different sectors such as automotive, consumer, industrial, medical , military , telecommunications and other. For almost 60 years, Karl Kruse has been known for their experience and market knowledge, application support as well as our pro-active service and commitment to quality.

Maxtena, Inc. is excited to be signed on with Karl Kruse GmbH & Co KG. Having Karl Kruse GmbH & Co KG as a distributor will help Maxtena, Inc. assist their broad international customer base. "We are excited to have gained Karl Kruse GmbH & Co KG as a partner and we look forward to be able to provide our cutting edge technology to new markets," said Maxtena's Sales & Marketing Manager, Vanja Marić.

Maxtena® is a research, development, and manufacturing company offering both standard and custom antenna products for a variety of applications with an emphasis on Mobile Satellite Services and high performance GPS antennas. Maxtena® offers Helicore® Technology, Precision GPS (L1/L2), and Smart-X™ phased-array antennas (12dBi performance with Omni-directional coverage) with 3-axis stabilization for global asset tracking. Maxtena® provides customer-tailored research for antenna element design, antenna array design, propagation effects, system design and engineering services.
