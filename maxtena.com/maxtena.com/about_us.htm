Dr. Stanislav Licul
Chief Executive Officer
Dr. Licul is President and Chief Executive Officer of Maxtena, Inc. He is responsible for research, development, and manufacturing of innovative antenna technologies based on differential interface and advanced materials. His research encompasses ultra-wideband antennas, multi-band antennas, embedded antennas, phased array antenna systems, RFIC-antenna integration techniques, and antenna measurement techniques. Prior to founding Maxtena, Dr. Licul served as Chief Scientist of Zylaya Corp. focusing on various antenna and wireless propagation aspects of wireless sensor networks. Dr. Licul also served as a research engineer with Motorola EME Corporate Labs focusing on cellular phone technologies in the areas of advanced antenna design and integration. Dr. Licul received his Ph.D. in electrical engineering from Virginia Tech. During the course of his studies, he interned with Motorola and XM Satellite Radio. His duties included optimization of the paging/satellite receivers and design of antennas for satellite and mobile communications. He is a member of Eta Kappa Nu and IEEE. Dr. Licul holds two patents and has three patent pending applications.
Dr. Carlo Di Nallo
Chief Technology Officer
Dr. Carlo Di Nallo received a Laurea Degree (Summa cum Laude) in Electronic Engineering and a Doctoral Degree in Applied Electromagnetics from the University of Rome “La Sapienza”, Italy. He worked as a consultant for different companies in Italy designing, modeling and characterizing phased arrays and large reflector antennas. Later he was with Telespazio S.p.A as a communication system engineer working on the Astrolink project. In 2000 he joined Motorola Labs where he was responsible for investigating and providing antenna and RF solutions for the next generations of Motorola products.
In the years with Motorola he significantly contributed to the advancement of the state of the art in multi-band embedded antenna technologies and his ideas have been successfully implemented in millions of products.
Dr. Di Nallo holds 8 patents and has 6 patents pending. He is the author of more than 20 journal papers and over 50 conference papers on numerical modeling, leaky wave antennas, radiation and crosstalk phenomena in printed circuits, dielectric resonator antennas and filters, propagation and radiation in anisotropic media, phased arrays, novel antenna structures, compact multiband antennas and tunable antennas.
Dr. Di Nallo is a member of IEEE.
Dr. Nathan P. Cummings
Vice President of Engineering
Dr. Cummings holds a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Virginia Tech. His antenna research interests include compact and low-profile designs, reconfigurable and active designs along with integrated and conformal phased arrays. Dr. Cummings has also been heavily involved in the area of electromagnetic interference analysis and interference mitigation techniques. Prior to joining Maxtena, Dr. Cummings held the positions of Senior Scientist at Sciperio, Inc and Senior Principal Engineer at ARINC, Inc. He has served as technical lead and collaborated with a wide range of commercial, military and agency customers including the U.S. Army, Navy, Air Force, Coast Guard, SOCOM, DARPA, MDA and others. Dr. Cummings is a member of the IEEE.
Wayne Telman
Chief Operations Officer
Mr. Telman holds an MBA from Troy State University and a BSBA from the University of the State of New York- Albany. He is a graduate of the US Navy Nuclear Power School and the US Navy Electrical Engineering (ETB) School. He has held several positions in senior management including Senior Director of Manufacturing and System Quality Assurance for TeraHop Networks, Inc., Senior VP and General Manager of Gray Laboratories, Inc., and Staff Program Manager for Scientific Atlanta, Inc. In his 30+ years in satellite communications, Mr. Telman has held P&L responsibility in Fortune 500 as well as startup companies. His professional areas of accomplishment include market definition and development, turnaround management, profitability recovery, revenue growth, and domestic/international customer relations.
